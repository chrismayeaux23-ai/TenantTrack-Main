# Loom Demo Script — 90 Seconds

Goal: A single 90-second screen recording that shows tenant → QR → vendor → done. Embeddable in DMs, emails, and the `/landlords` page. Hosted on Loom (free tier is fine).

## Recording setup

- Browser zoom: 110% — text needs to read on a phone screen
- Use the demo account (pre-seeded data) so the dashboard isn't empty
- Have two browser windows ready: your landlord dashboard, and a phone-sized window simulating the tenant
- Loom: webcam bubble ON (small, bottom-right) — your face on camera doubles play-through rate
- Background noise: silent. Do one practice take, then record.

## The script (read this, don't memorize — natural is better)

**[0:00 – 0:05] Hook**
> "If you self-manage rentals, this is the 90 seconds that's going to give you back your nights and weekends."

**[0:05 – 0:20] Tenant side — show the QR scan**
- Show a printed flyer on screen / hold up a phone
> "Your tenant has a leak. They scan the QR code on their fridge. No app, no account, no password reset email. They pick the issue, type two sentences, snap a photo, hit send."
- Click through the public form on the phone-sized window. Submit it.

**[0:20 – 0:35] Landlord side — show the request landing**
- Cut to your dashboard. The request appears.
> "On your end, the request is in your dashboard the moment they hit send — with photos, urgency, and the unit. You also got the same thing as an email so you don't have to be staring at the screen."

**[0:35 – 0:55] Dispatch — show auto-dispatch**
- Open the request, hit auto-dispatch
> "Now here's the part that changes the math. Every vendor in your network has a trust score — based on whether they show up, whether they finish, and whether your tenants liked the work. One click, the highest-scoring plumber for this trade gets a magic link in their email. No login, no app for them either."

**[0:55 – 1:15] Vendor accepts — show vendor portal**
- Open the magic link in another tab, accept
> "They tap accept. You get notified. Tenant gets notified. The job is on the calendar. When it's done, the vendor uploads the invoice right there. The whole thing — request to invoice — lives on one page."

**[1:15 – 1:30] Close — soft CTA**
> "If you have ten to fifty units and you're tired of being the maintenance dispatch line — try the 14-day trial. I'll personally help you set up your first property over a 30-minute call. Link is in [the description / my message]. Thanks."

## Re-record if

- You said "um" more than 3 times → re-record
- You went over 1:45 → re-record (cut 15 seconds, the close is too long)
- The audio level was inconsistent → re-record
- You didn't show the email + dashboard split → re-record (this is the wow moment)

Don't re-record because you didn't like your hair. Done > perfect.

## After recording

1. Set Loom video privacy to "Anyone with the link"
2. Custom thumbnail: a frame showing both the email AND the dashboard side-by-side
3. Title: "TenantTrack — 90 seconds"
4. Copy the share URL — paste it into:
   - The `<iframe>` src on the `/landlords` page (replace the placeholder Loom ID — see code comment)
   - Every outreach template in `outreach-templates.md`
   - Your email signature
